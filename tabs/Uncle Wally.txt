Alright, Uncle Wally:

During the intro, Joel is playing these pairs of notes, not chords:

B-C# : G#-A# : E-F#

Under that, Pony is playing these pairs:

D#-E : B-C# : E-F#
So, it would seem that they're moving through these pairs of chords:

Bmaj-C#m : G#m-F#7/A# : E-F#

These chords are really only suggested, not overtly played, but if you want to play
